Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the expense tracking features of Spendly. This includes displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and filtering by date or category. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the service for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in a local database associated with your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorised access. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your profile settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with any third-party services, advertisers, or analytics providers. Any external libraries or fonts used by the app (such as Google Fonts) operate independently and are not given access to your account data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out through the app's support channel. We are committed to addressing any privacy concerns promptly.
